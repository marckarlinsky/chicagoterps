Yelling at TV screens together since 2012

Deep in Big Ten country, Maryland fans have had a place to watch the big ones for over a decade.

We organize game watches for Maryland football and basketball — reliably for rivalry games, ranked matchups, March, and bowl season, and often for the rest. Turnout runs anywhere from a handful of diehards to a packed room, so the schedule lives on Instagram: check @chicagoterpsalumni to see which games have a watch and confirm before you head out.
